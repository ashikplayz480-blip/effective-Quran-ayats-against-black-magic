# 📿 Ruqyah Shariyah (الرقية الشرعية) – Islamic Healing Guide

A comprehensive, step-by-step guide to performing authentic Ruqyah (spiritual healing) according to the Quran and Sunnah.

---

## 🌟 What is Ruuqyah?

**Ruqyah (رُقية)** is the Islamic practice of reciting Quranic verses and authentic supplications to seek healing from spiritual and physical ailments. It is a Sunnah taught by Prophet Muhammad ﷺ and Angel Jibril (AS).

&gt; *"And We send down of the Quran that which is healing and mercy for the believers."*  
&gt; — **Quran 17:82**

---

## 📋 Step-by-Step Ruqyah Protocol

### Step 1: The Opening – Surah Al-Fatihah

**The Greatest Surah for Healing**

- **📖 Reference:** Al-Fatihah (Chapter 1), Verses 1–7
- **Text:** *"Alhamdu lillahi Rabbil 'alamin..."*
- **✨** Recite with full conviction, as this surah is the most powerful remedy for all ailments.

### Step 2: The Throne Verse – Ayatul Kursi

**Protection from Shaytan & All Harm**

- **📖 Reference:** Al-Baqarah (Chapter 2), Verse 255
- **Text:** *"Allahu la ilaha illa huwa al-hayyu al-qayyum..."*
- **✨** The greatest verse of the Quran for protection against evil, jinn, and all forms of harm.

### Step 3: The Seal of Al-Baqarah

**Nightly Protection & Sufficiency**

- **📖 Reference:** Al-Baqarah (Chapter 2), Verses 285–286
- **Text:** *"Amana ar-rasulu bima unzila ilayhi min rabbihi wal-mu'minun..."*
- **✨** These two verses were given to the Prophet ﷺ from under the Throne. Reciting them at night provides sufficient protection.

### Step 4: The Four Quls (Al-Mu'awwidhat)

**Comprehensive Protection & Purification**

| Surah | Chapter | Purpose |
|-------|---------|---------|
| **Al-Kafirun** | 109 | Disavowal of shirk & purity of faith |

| **Al-Falaq** | 113 | Protection from evil creations, envy, and darkness |
| **An-Nas** | 114 | Protection from whisperings, possession, and hidden evils |

**✨ Method:** Recite each surah 3 times, blow gently into your cupped hands, and wipe over your entire body.

## 🛠️ How to Use This Guide

### 🔹 For Self-Ruqyah

1. **Prepare:** Make wudu (ablution), face the Qibla if possible, and sit in a clean, quiet place.
2. **Recite:** Follow Steps 1–4 daily as a minimum practice.
3. **Blow:** After reciting each section, blow gently into your cupped hands and wipe over your body—starting from head to feet.
4. **Water Method:** For Steps 5–8, recite the verses into a glass of water, then drink it or use it for bathing.
5. **Consistency:** Perform daily for 7, 40, or 90 days depending on the severity of the condition.

### 🔹 For Treating Others

1. Have the patient sit comfortably, facing the Qibla.
2. Place your right hand gently on their forehead or the affected area.
3. Recite the verses clearly and with conviction; loud recitation is permissible.
4. Observe for physical or emotional reactions (yawning, burping, crying, trembling, etc.), which may indicate the presence of spiritual affliction.
5. Always begin and end with Bismillah and send salutations upon the Prophet ﷺ.

## ⚠️ Important Conditions for Valid Ruqyah

### ✅ Requirements for Authentic Ruqyah

- **Belief:** Firm conviction that only Allah heals; Ruqyah is merely a means.
- **Language:** Recitation must be in clear, comprehensible Arabic (Quran or authentic supplications).
- **Source:** Use only Quranic verses and authentic Sunnah supplications.
- **Intention:** Pure intention seeking Allah's pleasure and healing.
- **Method:** No shirk, no haram elements, no innovations.

### ❌ What Invalidates Ruqyah

- Wearing amulets, taweez, or charms (even if they contain Quran).
- Visiting fortune-tellers, sorcerers, or self-proclaimed "healers" who use jinn.
- Mixing Ruqyah with bid'ah (innovations), music, or un-Islamic practices.
- Charging money for performing Ruqyah (though accepting voluntary gifts is permissible).
- Using unknown words, chants, or languages whose meaning is unclear.
